When to put your bins out

Blue & Green bins (Wed): Put out Tuesday evening or by 6am Wednesday
Brown bin (Mon): Put out Sunday evening or by 6am Monday
Black bin (glass): Put out Tuesday evening before collection day

📌

Bank holidays, Christmas & Easter

Collection dates may change during bank holidays, Christmas and Easter periods. Always check bassetlaw.gov.uk for the official schedule during these times.
